Our Story

Sushi 101 is a restaurant which simulates the feeling of dining in an authentic Japanese Restaurant.

We have a beautiful Sakura Tree in the dining place, where you can relax and savour your delicious food while enjoying the wonders of the tree.

Although our restaurant is newly opened, our head chef has been in the Sushi business for over 15 years, throughout which he has acquired tremendous skill, experience, and mastery of the art that is Sushi preparation. Dining at Sushi 101 is truly a once-in-a-lifetime culinary experience.
